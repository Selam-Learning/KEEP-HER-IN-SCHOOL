About Our Mission

» Serve Ethiopia is a student-based youth club under Selam Learning, founded in 2023.

In Serve Ethiopia we foster leadership through community service. We plan and organize volunteer schedules, collaborating with different charity organizations to make a meaningful impact. Our aim is to enrich the service experience for youths who are passionate about serving their community. Our priority organizations include Ethiopian Association of the Blind and Wogene foundation. We also have special projects such as the ENAB cleaning project and Keep Her In School.

» Keep Her In School

is a special intiative led by Serve Ethiopia to provide menstrual care products Blind/Visually impaired middle and high school students in Addis Ababa. Keep Her In School officially launched on December 21st, 2024 and will end on March 30th, 2025. The fundraising campaign will last for 3 months and will be executed primarily through selling tickets.
